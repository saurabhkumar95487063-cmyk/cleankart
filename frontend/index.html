Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, place an order, or communicate with us. This includes your name, phone number, email, and address.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to process orders, improve our service, and communicate updates about your laundry status.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. Your payment information is processed securely through Razorpay.

Terms of Service
1. Service Usage

By using CleanKart, you agree to provide accurate information and follow our ordering process.

2. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 15 minutes of placement without any charge. After pickup, cancellation is not possible.

3. Responsibility for Items

While we take maximum care, CleanKart is not responsible for color bleeding or shrinkage of clothes that are not dry-clean only.

Refund Policy
1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are applicable if an order is cancelled within the allowed time frame or if there is a verified damage during our process.

2. Process

Verified refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 working days.

3. Wallet Credits

In some cases, we may offer refund as CleanKart Wallet credits for instant use.
